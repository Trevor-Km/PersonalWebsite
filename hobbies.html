In January 2023, my foray into coding commenced unexpectedly, driven by the uncertainty surrounding my choice of a major. The decision to embrace coding emerged almost serendipitously, as I found myself at the crossroads of indecision. However, this fortuitous introduction quickly transformed into a purposeful journey, sparking a newfound passion for technology and problem-solving. Motivated by the intricate depth and technicality of Computer Science, I discovered a captivating world of complex algorithms, systematic approaches to problem-solving, and the limitless potential for technological innovation. This fascination became the catalyst for my pursuit of Computer Science, propelling me into a realm where challenges are met with enthusiasm, and the dynamic evolution of the field serves as an ongoing source of inspiration.

Basketball has been an enduring part of my life, woven into the fabric of my existence since childhood. The inspiration for my love of the game stems from my older brother, whose passion and skill on the court ignited my own. Beyond the physicality of the sport, basketball has served as a powerful means of self-expression, especially during my younger years when connecting with others posed challenges. The court became a sanctuary, a place where I could articulate myself through the language of the game. The most satisfying aspect of basketball, for me, lies in the collective pursuit of victory. There's a unique joy in witnessing the synergy that unfolds when individuals come together, each contributing their skills and efforts toward a common goal. In those moments of triumph, the shared commitment to success resonates, forging bonds and creating a sense of unity that transcends the boundaries of the court. The essence of basketball, as a team sport, encapsulates the beauty of collaboration and the fulfillment derived from working harmoniously towards achievement.
